How it works

Create groups

Set up a group for friends, family, or colleagues and keep all shared expenses in one place.

Whether you're planning a trip, sharing rent, or tracking everyday costs, everything stays organized.

Invite friends

Invite others using QR code or link and start sharing expenses in seconds.

Add real or virtual members so every balance stays fair, even when someone is not in the app yet.

Add transactions

Record who paid what with multiple currencies and always stay on top of your payments.

Great for trips abroad, shared rent, dinners, and every messy group expense in between.

Split the bills

ChopChop automatically calculates who owes whom and splits the bill fairly for you.

No spreadsheets, no awkward math, no endless chat messages after dinner.

No credit card required
