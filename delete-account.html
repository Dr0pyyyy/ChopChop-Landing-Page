Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 31, 2026

You can delete your ChopChop account and all associated data directly from the app. Follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the ChopChop app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon in the bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your personal information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Your IBAN / bank account number
  • Your authentication tokens and login credentials
  • Your push notification settings and device tokens
  • Bug reports you submitted

What May Be Retained

  • Transaction history within groups may be retained in anonymized form so that other group members' records remain accurate.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account or need assistance with deletion, contact us at chopchop.devv@gmail.com and we will process your request.
